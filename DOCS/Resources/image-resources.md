## üé® IMAGE SPECIFICATIONS

### Recommended Image Sizes:

**Disease Detection:**
- Resolution: 1024x1024 or higher
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Max size: 5MB (for Gemini API compatibility)

**Profile Pictures:**
- Resolution: 400x400
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Max size: 500KB

**Featured Images:**
- Resolution: 1920x1080 (16:9 aspect ratio)
- Format: JPG
- Max size: 2MB

**Thumbnails:**
- Resolution: 300x300
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Max size: 200KB

**App Icons:**
- Resolution: 512x512
- Format: PNG with transparency
- Max size: 500KB
